# 🛒 Supermarket Sales Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing supermarket sales data to uncover business insights related to revenue, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, and operational trends.

The analysis was performed using SQL for data querying, Python for exploratory data analysis (EDA), and Power BI for interactive dashboard creation.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

The primary objectives of this project were:

* Analyze branch-wise and city-wise sales performance
* Identify top-performing product categories
* Understand customer purchasing behavior
* Evaluate profitability across product lines
* Analyze payment preferences and customer ratings
* Identify peak sales periods and revenue trends
* Generate actionable business recommendations

## 💡 Key Findings

* Certain product categories generated a significant share of total revenue.
* Revenue performance varied across branches.
* Customer spending behavior differed across segments.
* A small percentage of transactions contributed disproportionately to total revenue.
* Peak sales occurred during specific business hours.
* Customer ratings remained consistently positive across branches.

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

* Increase inventory allocation for top-performing product categories.
* Focus marketing campaigns on high-value customer segments.
* Optimize staffing during peak business hours.
* Promote profitable product lines through targeted offers.
* Replicate best-performing branch strategies across locations.
